1. The rule of game:
Control Snake to eat all Foods in area. If Snake hits the wall or its body, You will lost. 
A MessageBox will appear and inform your spending time and points. Each Food is 1 point.

2. How to Play
Direction by:
W: Up
S: Down
D: Right
A: Left

3.Features:
- Adjust Size of Area (Width & Height)
- Adjust Delay: The smaller the number, the faster the speed
- Timer: count playing time
- Score: count points while play game
- Disable Playbutton,kinds of spinBox after click play
